Lifetime means as long as SignSimple.io exists and operates. You get permanent access to all features included in the LTD, plus all future updates to those features. If we ever discontinue the product (unlikely), we'll give you at least 12 months notice and a full data export.

The LTD includes generous limits for personal and small business use: up to 100 documents/month, 500 signatures/month, and 10GB storage. This covers 99% of users. If you need more, you can add capacity for a small fee. See our Fair Use Policy.

What if you add features later?

All improvements and updates to existing features are included forever. If we create entirely new product tiers (like Enterprise API access), those may be separate. But core functionality? That's yours for life.
