Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Purge and Maintenance?

One-Time Purge ($15) gives you 30 days of unlimited deletes - perfect for a deep clean. Maintenance ($5/mo) keeps your inbox clean forever with auto-rules and weekly reports.

Can I cancel Maintenance anytime?

Yes! Cancel with one click. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No contracts, no hassle.

What happens after my 30-day Purge expires?

You'll return to the free tier with 100 deletes/month. You can always buy another Purge or upgrade to Maintenance when you need more cleaning power.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use Google's official OAuth and never store your emails. All processing happens in real-time. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
